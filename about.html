About Us

Team 2 Bank is the latest founded bank in Singapore. We provide young customers aged 17-20 with Financial Knowledge.

Key Features

Our digital wallet offers a wide variety of features tailored to the needs of young customers, such as:

  • Financial Education: Interactive games to teach financial literacy.
  • Budgeting Tools: Tools to help track spendings and manage budget.
  • Parental Controls: For parents to monitor their children's financial activities.

Why Choose Us?

We believe that educating youths on financial literacy is crucial because their spending habits are formed early in life. By providing them with the knowledge and tools to manage their money effectively, we can help them build a strong financial foundation that will benefit them throughout their lives. Understanding how to budget, save, and spend wisely empowers young adults to make informed financial decisions, avoid debt, and achieve their financial goals.
