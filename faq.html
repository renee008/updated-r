Youth Banking App FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the home page and fill in your details. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the registration process.

How do I transfer money to another account?

To transfer money, log in to your account, go to the "Transfers" section, and follow the instructions to complete the transfer. You will need the recipient's account number and bank details.

Is my personal information secure?

We take your privacy and security seriously. Our app uses advanced encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.
